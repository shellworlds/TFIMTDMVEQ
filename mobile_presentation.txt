QUANTUM THERMODYNAMICS SOFTWARE
================================

SLIDE 1: PROBLEM STATEMENT
--------------------------
Classical computation of quantum thermodynamics faces exponential scaling.
Example: N-spin system has 2^N quantum states.
50 spins = 1.12e15 states (computationally prohibitive).

SLIDE 2: QUANTUM SOLUTION
-------------------------
Quantum computers use superposition to represent states efficiently.
Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE) finds ground states.
Our implementation: Transverse Field Ising Model.

SLIDE 3: TECHNICAL ACHIEVEMENT
------------------------------
* Working POC with Qiskit quantum framework
* Validated against classical exact diagonalization
* 2-4 qubit systems demonstrated
* Ready for hardware deployment

SLIDE 5: RESEARCH CHALLENGES
----------------------------
* Errors increase with system size (ansatz limitation)
* Need finite temperature algorithms
* Requires error mitigation for hardware
* Scaling to 8+ qubits needed
